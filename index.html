What is TheoCompass?

A hub for careful theological exploration

TheoCompass is a project designed to help you explore the vast and diverse world of Christian theology in a nuanced and insightful way. It goes beyond typical quizzes by analyzing not just what you believe, but also how you believe it—your certainty and your tolerance for other views.

The goal is not to tell you “what you are,” but to provide a detailed map of your unique theological profile and show you how it relates to more than 230 Christian denominations and movements. This tool is built on a transparent, data-driven scoring model designed to be as fair and objective as possible.
