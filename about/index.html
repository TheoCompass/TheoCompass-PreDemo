Mission

Why TheoCompass exists

TheoCompass exists to help people explore Christian theology with clarity, charity, and intellectual honesty. The project aims to make theological self-understanding more informed, less reactionary, and more transparent.

Rather than forcing users into simplistic labels, TheoCompass tries to map patterns of belief with nuance. It pays attention not only to doctrinal positions, but also to posture—how strongly a belief is held and how open a user is toward other views.

What makes it different

  • Nuanced scoring instead of one-dimensional labels.
  • Transparent methodology and data-driven matching.
  • A charitable and neutral tone across traditions.
  • Results designed for learning, not pressure.

Current phase

The pre-demo focuses on the Denomination Alignment Quiz v2.0. Over time, this hub can grow into a broader collection of tools, explainers, and community-driven resources.
